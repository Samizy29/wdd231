Bamace Bag - Site Plan

1. Site Name

Bamace Bag - This is the name of my mum's bag business. The site will represent her brand and help promote her products online.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase and sell various types of bags including school bags, traveling bags, and more. It will allow customers to browse the collection and place orders directly.

3. Scenarios

  • Where can I find quality and affordable school bags for my kids?
  • Do you sell durable traveling bags that can be delivered to Lagos mainland?

4. Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #0E2F44 (Dark Blue) - Used for headers, navigation bar, and call-to-action buttons.
  • Secondary Color: #FFD700 (Golden Yellow) - Used for highlights, links, and button hovers.

5. Typography

  • Font: Open Sans
  • Used for body text and headings for a clean and professional look.
